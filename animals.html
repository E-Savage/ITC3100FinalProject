Animal kingdom facts

Living organisms are placed in groups based on similarities. Biological classification begins with a general classification and becomes more specific as the classifications are broken down further.

The eight levels inlcude:

  • Domain (Super-kingdom)
  • Kingdom
  • Phylum
  • Class
  • Order
  • Family
  • Genus
  • Species
