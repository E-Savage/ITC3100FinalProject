Fun Facts about Alligators

  • Alligators can go through 3,000 teeth in a lifetime. Imagine if they had to pay for dental insurance!
  • Alligator temperature is determined by water temperature.
  • Contrary to widespread belief, alligators are more scared of you than you might be of them!
