Octopus Fun Facts

  • Octopuses are smarter than they look. They can remember and recognize individual humans.
  • Octopuses have a similar birthing method to chickens- they lay eggs and give birth to fully developed young.
  • Octopuses are not only sea creatures; they can run on land.
