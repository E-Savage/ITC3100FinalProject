Red Panda fun facts

  • Red pandas have similar grooming habits to house cats and lick themselves clean.
  • Red pandas stay warm in colder environments by wrapping their tails around themselves.
  • Red pandas are herbivories and eat about 20,000 bamboo leaves every day.
